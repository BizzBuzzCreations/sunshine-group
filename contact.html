Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Have a question before reaching out? Browse our most frequently asked questions below. If you can't find your answer, our team is just a call away.

Call Us
How do I book a free consultation with Sunshine Group?

Simply fill out the contact form on this page, call us directly at +91 9554953833 and +91 9554953834, or WhatsApp us. Our team will schedule a free 60-minute consultation at your preferred time and location.

Are all Sunshine Group projects RERA registered?

Yes, absolutely. All our projects are fully registered under the Real Estate (Regulation and Development) Act, 2016 (RERA). We are fully compliant with all regulatory requirements.

Do you assist with home loan facilitation?

Yes. We have partnerships with leading banks and NBFCs including HDFC, SBI, ICICI, and Axis Bank. Our finance team assists with loan applications, EMI planning, and documentation at no additional charge to you.

Can NRI investors purchase Sunshine Group properties?

Absolutely. We have a dedicated NRI desk to assist with cross-border transactions, FEMA compliance, power of attorney documentation, and repatriation of funds. We serve NRI clients from the UAE, UK, USA, Canada, Australia, and beyond.

What is the typical response time after submitting an enquiry?

We typically respond to all online enquiries within 2-4 business hours. For WhatsApp messages, we aim to respond within 30 minutes during business hours. Urgent queries are always welcome via our direct phone line.

Do you offer virtual site visits for outstation clients?

Yes. We offer high-definition virtual tours via video call for all our ongoing and completed projects. Our sales team can guide you through the property in real-time, answer questions, and share documentation digitally — all from the comfort of your home.
