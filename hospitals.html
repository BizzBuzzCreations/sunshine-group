About the Project

A New Era of Healthcare in Prayagraj

NEOLIFE Hospital & Diagnostic Centre is an upcoming state-of-the-art healthcare destination being developed by Sunshine Group in the heart of Naini, Prayagraj.

Designed as an integrated healthcare campus, the project combines a modern Diagnostic Centre with a fully equipped 100+ bed Multispecialty Hospital, creating a seamless ecosystem for diagnosis, treatment, surgery, critical care, and recovery.

Built with a vision to bring world-class healthcare infrastructure to the region, NEOLIFE will feature advanced medical technology, specialized departments, renowned consultants, and patient-centric facilities that redefine healthcare standards across Prayagraj and surrounding districts.
