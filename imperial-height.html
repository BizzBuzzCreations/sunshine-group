About the Project

Sunshine Imperial Heights — Redefining Modern Living in Prayagraj

Sunshine Imperial Heights is a premium residential township thoughtfully designed to offer a world-class lifestyle in one of Prayagraj's fastest-growing locations. Situated on the prime Dhanuha–Rewa Road corridor, the development combines contemporary architecture, spacious residences, modern amenities, and exceptional connectivity to create a truly elevated living experience.

As Prayagraj continues to expand with new flyovers, improved infrastructure, and growing urban development, homebuyers and investors are increasingly seeking well-planned communities that provide comfort, convenience, and long-term value. Sunshine Imperial Heights stands at the forefront of this transformation.

Project Highlights
  • 16,000+ Sq. Meters of Prime Development
    A meticulously planned township spread across a vast land parcel, offering generous open spaces and a balanced living environment.
  • 422 Premium Residences
    Spaciously designed 2 & 3 BHK apartments crafted to maximize comfort, natural light, ventilation, and functionality.
  • 6 Residential Blocks across 2 Towers
    Tower 1: Block A, B, C  |  Tower 2: Block D, E, F — well-planned towers organized for privacy, accessibility, and community living.
  • 8,50,000+ Sq. Ft. of Development
