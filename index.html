Why Invest in Prayagraj?

The eternal city of Sangam is India's fastest-growing tier-2 investment hub — and Sunshine Group is your trusted gateway to its most exclusive properties.

Maha Kumbh 2025 — The World's Largest Gathering 45 Crore+ pilgrims visited, transforming Prayagraj into a globally recognised city. Infrastructure worth ₹7,000+ Crore was developed. Real estate demand has skyrocketed — and Sunshine Group is at the heart of it all.
Real Estate Boom

The Sacred Sangam

The confluence of Ganga, Yamuna & Saraswati draws millions of pilgrims and tourists year-round, making Prayagraj one of India's most consistently in-demand property markets.

World-Class Infrastructure

New expressways, an expanded international airport, metro rail plans, and Yamuna Expressway connectivity — Prayagraj is undergoing a ₹25,000+ Crore infrastructure transformation.

Education & Government Hub

Home to IIT, Allahabad University, NIT, High Court & key government institutions — the city attracts students, professionals, and executives, fuelling strong rental demand.

18%+ Annual Appreciation

Post-Kumbh infrastructure boom has accelerated property values across Prayagraj. Strategic micro-markets are delivering returns that outpace saturated metro cities.

Smart City Initiative

Prayagraj is a Smart City Mission beneficiary with ₹2,000+ Crore allocated for urban renewal, green corridors, and smart infrastructure — driving long-term property appreciation.

Tourism & Heritage Boom

Anand Bhawan, Khusro Bagh, Triveni Sangam — a city rich in history and spirituality draws 10 Crore+ annual visitors, making hospitality and commercial real estate highly lucrative.
