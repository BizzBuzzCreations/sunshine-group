Our Story

From Humble Beginnings to Industry Leadership

For over 38 years, Sunshine Group has been shaping the future of Prayagraj through thoughtfully planned residential, commercial, and infrastructure developments. Founded on the principles of integrity, quality, transparency, and customer satisfaction, the Group has earned the trust of thousands of families, investors, and stakeholders across the region.
Established by visionary entrepreneur Mr. Hemant Kumar Sindhi, Sunshine Group began its journey in infrastructure and government contracting, successfully executing over 100 projects across multiple states before expanding into real estate development. This rich legacy of engineering excellence and project execution forms the foundation of the Group’s reputation today.
From premium residential communities and modern commercial landmarks to large-scale township developments, Sunshine Group continues to create spaces that enrich lifestyles, strengthen communities, and deliver lasting value for generations.

A Word From Our Founder

38 Years of Excellence in Infrastructure, Construction & Real Estate.
“Building Trust, Creating Landmarks, Shaping Communities.”

Renowned for his visionary leadership and exceptional business acumen, Mr. Hemant Kumar Sindhi is the driving force behind Sunshine Group’s enduring success. Beginning his professional journey as a government contractor, he successfully executed over 100 infrastructure and construction projects across various regions, establishing a reputation for reliability, quality, and timely execution. Guided by strong values and a long-term vision, he laid the foundation of Sunshine Group on the principles of integrity, transparency, and customer trust.
Over the decades, he transformed the organization into one of Prayagraj’s respected real estate developers, known for delivering quality developments and creating lasting relationships with customers and stakeholders.
His philosophy extends beyond constructing buildings—it is rooted in creating vibrant communities, enhancing lifestyles, and delivering developments that stand the test of time. Today, his visionary guidance continues to inspire Sunshine Group’s pursuit of excellence, innovation, and sustainable growth.
